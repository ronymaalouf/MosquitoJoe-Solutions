Mixing Rules
Mix Order
Always mix in separate container. Surf-AC FIRST, shake well, then add to blower.
Blower Fill Order
Water first Add mixed container Top off with water

IGR Rule
When fleas or ticks in the mix, Tekko Pro replaces Pivot 10 entirely
Chemical Reference
Surf-AC
Surfactant. Always first. 10 ml/gal always.
Bifen
Primary synthetic insecticide. 1 oz/gal. Do NOT use if respray within 7 days.
Pivot 10
IGR for mosquitoes, gnats, stink bugs, chiggers. 56 ml/gal. Replaced by Tekko Pro when fleas/ticks present.
Tekko Pro
IGR for fleas & ticks. 1 oz/gal. Replaces Pivot 10.
EcoVia MT
Botanical knockdown. 0.5 oz/gal.
EcoVia EC
Concentrated botanical. Fleas Only / Red Mites. 2 oz/gal.
Stop The Bites
Botanical repellent. Natural treatment + respray within 7 days. 4 oz/gal normal, 6 oz/gal heavy.
Stryker
Fast knockdown. Optional 10 ml/gal only if you see a lot of flyers.
Fuse
Non-repellent insecticide for ants. 0.5 oz/gal.
InTice 10
Ant bait granules. 23 ft from foundation. NOT on concrete.
Tempo Dust
50/50 with EcoVia WD for stinging insect nests.
Pro-Pell
Rodent repellent. 12 oz/gal. 1 ft band around foundation.
Larvicides
Altosid Granules
1 tsp per 50 ft water up to 2 ft deep. NOT drinking water.
Altosid Briquettes
1 per 100 ft water up to 2 ft deep.
Mosquito Dunks
1 per 1,500 ft moving water. Safe for fish.
